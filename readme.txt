==============================
SmellBounty
==============================

To compile contract and migrate on Truffle develop

	>truffle develop
	>migrate

To setup contract

	>exec setup.js
	
	
To cancel contract and get bounty back

	>exec cancel.js
	
	
To check status of contract

	>exec status.js
	
	
To trigger the smell check

	>exec didfix.js


To access account balance check shorthand

	>exec balances.js
